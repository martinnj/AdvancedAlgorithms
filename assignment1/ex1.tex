\section{Exercise 1: $b$-flow}
A flow is a b-flow if its satisfies the following
\begin{align}
\sum_{e \in \delta^- (v)} x_e - \sum_{e \in \delta^+ (v)} x_e &= b_v, \forall v \in V \label{1}\\
0 \leq x_e &\leq u_e, \forall e \in E \label{2}
\end{align}

\noindent Below we have illustrated the $b$-flow for figure (a). We see that each node satisfies equation \ref{1} and \ref{2}, giving us the $b$-flow illustrated. \\
